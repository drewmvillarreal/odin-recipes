Fajita Tacos

Ingredients

  • Corn or Flour Tortillas
  • Beef or Chicken Fajita
  • Avocados
  • Mexican Cheese
  • Refried Beans
  • Rice
  • Limes
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the fajita meat on medium high heat until desired browness.
  2. In a separate skillet, warm up the refried beans.
  3. Cook rice in a rice cooker or pot with caldo de pollo.
  4. Cook tortillas in a small pan one at a time.
  5. Cut the avocados and limes and mix to make homemade guacamole with desired spices.
  6. Plate your tortillas and make your tacos.
  7. Optional: Garnish with cheese, guacamole, cilantro, and salsa.
