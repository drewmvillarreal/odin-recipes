Oyakodon - Chicken Egg

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Eggs
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Dashi
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Chopped Onion
  • Rice

    • Steps

    1. Cook 1 cup of rice in either a rice cooker or pot.
    2. Cut chicken breast into bite sized pieces.
    3. In a large skillet, mix 1/3 cup of sake, mirin, dashi, and soy sauce along with the chopped onion and chicken breast and bring to a boil.
    4. Boil the mixture for 5 minutes.
    5. Lightly mix 3-4 eggs separately, then pour 3/4 of the egg on top of the mixture and cover with a lid.
    6. Let the egg cook for 1 minute, then add the ramining egg to the skillet and cover for 30 seconds.
    7. Garnish with green onion.
    8. Serve over the rice and enjoy!
